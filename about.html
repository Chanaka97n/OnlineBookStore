About A New Chapter


A group of web developers and an enthusiastic bookworm teamed up in 2017 and decided to make an online bookstore to make it convenient for people to get their favorite books.


What started as a hobby turned out to be one of the most visited online bookstores of 2018 and now New Chapter has started expanding it's business by opening up shops in certain locations and will attract more customers as the physical copy will always have an influence over a simple PDF. To learn more about us and the people working behind the scenes visit the about us page.


Our Values


MISSION: A New Chapter is an independent bookshop committed to offering a quality selection of affordable new, bargain, gently read, and electronic books for customers of all ages and backgrounds. We are passionate about the written word and seek to share our love of reading to help create and sustain a community of lifelong readers. We are committed to engaging with the many diverse peoples and organizations of our community, particularly on a national and international scale, to work towards the goals of a knowledgeable public and a peaceful and environmentally sustainable planet.


VISION: To establish a vibrant, independent community bookshop that is run for and by the local people who live, work and study in and around Kandy, Colombo and such major areas. The bookshop will not only ensure that local people can continue to have an independent bookshop in their high street, but also contribute to the growing movement to “bring back books”. The financial surplus from the bookshop will be invested back into community activities; the bookshop will be run as a not-for-private-profit venture and managed by a professional bookseller.
