A NEW CHAPTER

A group of web developers and an enthusiastic bookworm teamed up in 2017 and decided to make an online bookstore to make it convenient for people to get their favorite books. What started as a hobby turned out to be one of the most visited online bookstores of 2018 and now New Chapter has started expanding it's business by opening up shops in certain locations and will attract more customers as the physical copy will always have an influence over a simple PDF.
